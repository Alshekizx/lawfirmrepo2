Our firm has vast experience in the area of pre and post election matters. our firm has successfully prosecuted and defended various election matters in almost all the states of Nigeria ranging from local government election to the election for the office of the of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

the firm offers business regulatory and compliance advisory services,to both local and foriegn clients to assist them in establishing businesses in nigeria, Maintaining regulatory Compliance with statutory provision, Provide company secretarial services and regulatory filings. we have experienced Solicitors who are versed in obtaining regulatory permits/ approvals for both local and foreign companies to operate in nigeria including business permits,expatriats quotas, transfer of technology approvals, CERPAC Card, Work Permits, NAFDAC registration of Regulated Products,etc. Further to registration, we also manage legal entities.
